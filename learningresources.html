Learning Resources

While this is nowhere near ALL of the resources available out there, or even the ones that might be the best option for you, these are the main resources I used during my journey to learn coding and web development.

Learning Platforms

Udemy

Udemy was the very first platform I started on to learn to code. Specifically, The Complete 2022 Web Development Bootcamp, taught by Dr. Angela Yu. She was a phenomenal instructor, and made everything very easy to understand.

Sololearn

Sololearn offers a wide variety of courses for different coding languages. There is a little bit of something for everyone.

Codecademy

Codecademy, much like Sololearn, offers all kinds of programs for all kinds of programming languages and is an all-around excellent resource.
